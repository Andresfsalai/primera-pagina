WHO WE ARE?

At GE Electric, we’ve proudly served Northern Virginia since 2010, providing top-notch electrical services for both homes and businesses. Our team of skilled electricians is dedicated to ensuring your electrical systems are safe, efficient, and easy to use. With fully insured and guaranteed work, our priority is always exceeding your expectations and delivering results you can trust.

WHAT WE DO?

We offer a full range of residential and commercial electrical services, from new construction wiring to repairs and upgrades. Whether it’s lighting installations, panel upgrades, or troubleshooting, we get the job done right. Our expert team ensures safe, reliable solutions tailored to your needs – all backed by our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

OUR EXPERIENCE

With over a decade of experience, GE Electric is a trusted provider of electrical services for both residential and commercial projects. From new construction and home renovations to complex commercial installations, we handle it all with precision and care.

When you choose us, you’re choosing a team that ensures quality, safety, and customer satisfaction every time.
