FAQs

What services does GE Electric offer?

We offer a full range of electrical services for both residential and commercial properties, including new construction wiring, panel upgrades, lighting installations, and energy-efficient solutions.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, all our electricians are fully licensed, insured, and trained to ensure that your project is completed safely and professionally.

How can I get an estimate?

You can request a free estimate by contacting us via phone or through our online form. We'll schedule a consultation and provide an accurate estimate for your project.

How long does a project take to complete?

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project. We strive to complete every job efficiently and on time, keeping you updated throughout the process.

Does GE Electric offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer emergency electrical services for urgent repairs and electrical issues. Contact us anytime, and we’ll be there to assist you.

What types of electrical installations do you perform?

We handle all types of electrical installations, including new wiring, lighting setups, switch and outlet replacements, panel upgrades, and more for both homes and businesses.

Can I upgrade my electrical panel with GE Electric?

Absolutely! We specialize in panel upgrades and replacements to ensure your system meets safety standards and operates efficiently.

How do I know if my electrical system needs an upgrade?

If you're experiencing frequent power outages, flickering lights, or have an outdated panel, it may be time for an upgrade. Contact us for a thorough inspection and recommendations.

Do you offer outdoor lighting services?

Yes, we provide outdoor lighting installations such as landscape lights, security lighting, and more to enhance visibility and safety around your property.

Do you offer a warranty on your services?

Yes, all of our work is fully guaranteed. If you experience any issues after the job is completed, we'll address them at no additional cost to you.

Does GE Electric offer commercial services?

Yes, we specialize in commercial projects, including warehouse lighting, pole lighting, sign lighting, and other electrical solutions tailored to businesses.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

While we don’t offer regular discounts, we always strive to provide competitive pricing and exceptional value for every project. Our focus is on delivering quality service that exceeds your expectations.
